What is Adobe AI Hackathon?

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the hackathon? +
The hackathon is open to all students, developers, and innovators passionate about building AI solutions. Teams of 4 members are required to participate.
Do participants need to bring their own laptops? +
Yes, participants are required to bring their own laptops and any other hardware they may need for their project. Power outlets will be available at the venue.
Will food and refreshments be provided? +
Yes! Food and refreshments will be provided to all participants throughout the 24-hour hackathon, including dinner on Day 1 and breakfast on Day 2.
Is prior AI experience required to participate? +
No prior AI experience is required! The hackathon welcomes participants of all skill levels. Mentor sessions will be available to guide beginners throughout the event.
Can teams build projects before the event? +
Teams may plan and design concepts before the event, but all actual coding and development must begin only after the official hackathon start at 2:30 PM on 7 March. Pre-built or existing projects are not allowed.
